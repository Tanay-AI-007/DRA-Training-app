DRA Training Platform - Instructions

Follow these steps to access and complete your training smoothly.

Estimated read time: 1-2 minutes

1. Who is this for?

This platform is designed for DRA trainees (students) and Admin / Institutes.

2. How to Login

  1. 1Enter your registered email ID.
  2. 2Enter your password.
  3. 3Enter your Monthly Access Code (provided by your institute).
  4. 4Click LOGIN.
Access code is mandatory for course access.

3. What is Monthly Access Code?

The Monthly Access Code is issued by your institute. It is valid for a limited time (monthly) and is required to unlock the training content. Contact your institute if the access code is not working.

4. Features of the Platform

  • Trilingual learning - English / Hindi / Marathi
  • Banking-focused content (Recovery Laws, Case Studies & more)
  • Progress tracking and performance reports
  • IIBF-aligned preparation for professional excellence

5. Troubleshooting

  • Forgot Password? Use the Forgot Password option.
  • Invalid Access Code? Contact your institute.
  • Login issues? Check your internet connection and credentials.

6. Contact / Support

For any issues or assistance, please contact your training institute.
